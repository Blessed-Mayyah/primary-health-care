Our Services

At Primary Health Facility, we offer a wide range of healthcare services to meet the needs of our community.

Primary Care

Our primary care services focus on preventive care, health maintenance, and the treatment of common illnesses and injuries. Our team of primary care physicians and nurse practitioners provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages.

Preventive Care

We believe in the importance of preventive care to maintain optimal health and wellness. Our preventive care services include routine screenings, vaccinations, health education, and counseling to help patients prevent illness and disease.

Women's Health

We provide a range of women's health services, including routine gynecological exams, contraceptive counseling, prenatal care, and menopause management. Our compassionate and experienced healthcare providers are dedicated to addressing the unique healthcare needs of women at every stage of life.

Pediatric Care

Our pediatricians specialize in the care of infants, children, and adolescents. From well-child visits and immunizations to the treatment of childhood illnesses and developmental screenings, we are committed to providing high-quality pediatric care in a child-friendly environment.

Remote Doctor Consultations

Talking to a doctor has never been easier and more affordable. Get personalized, premium care when you speak to our licensed and experienced Medical Doctors. Our call centre is open 24/7 and you can consult in English and several local languages through chats, voice and video calls.

Laboratory

We know that not everyone wants to leave their homes when they are feeling unwell, our trained laboratory professionals are ready to come to your location when you need the service. The results are shared with you electronically after a detailed explanation by our Medical Doctors.

Pharmacy

Get your medicine and other pharmacy orders delivered within hours. We also deliver prescriptions for clients with chronic illnesses who need a regular supply of their medicine.

Clinic

Reduce your waiting time and access best-in-class care whenever you visit the Rocket Health Clinic.

Wellness Test

Take charge of your health with our range of wellness tests. Screening helps in early detection of certain preventable and controllable conditions such as high blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, stroke and coronary heart disease.

Chronic Care Management

We make it easy for you to manage ongoing chronic conditions like type-2-diabetes and hypertension. We offer monthly medicine refills, reminders for routine laboratory tests, reviews with a specialist and annual healthcare plans for optimum care management.

Insurance

Rocket Health accepts local and international medical insurance cards from ICEA, Liberty, Prudential, Sanlam, Jubilee, UAP Old Mutual, AAR, GA, Allianz, Bupa and Aetna Insurance.
